### [1] [A construction of surface skein TQFTs and their extension to 4-dimensional 2-handlebodies](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.19352)
*Leon J. Goertz*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 该论文为交换Frobenius代数A构建了一个(2,3,3+ε)维TQFT，通过表面skein模块研究3-流形上的A-装饰表面，并部分扩展到4维。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究交换Frobenius代数A的拓扑量子场论(TQFT)构造，特别是将Asaeda-Frohman和Kaiser定义的表面skein模块系统化，并探索其在更高维度（特别是4维）的扩展可能性。

Method: 1. 为交换Frobenius代数A构造(2,3,3+ε)维TQFT AFK_A；2. 对带边界的3-流形，证明表面skein模块携带由边界关联的表面skein范畴的作用，建立粘合形式体系；3. 使用Walker的归纳状态和构造将TQFT部分扩展到4维；4. 通过特定Kirby颜色表征4维2-把手的附着。

Result: 1. 成功构造了AFK_A TQFT；2. 建立了带边界3-流形的表面skein模块作用理论；3. 实现了TQFT到4维的部分扩展；4. 发现Lee变形的等变版本可扩展到4维2-把手体但不可扩展到3-把手体；5. 开发了计算4维2-把手体不变量的方法。

Conclusion: 该工作为交换Frobenius代数提供了系统的(2,3,3+ε)维TQFT框架，成功将表面skein模块理论扩展到带边界情形，并实现了到4维的部分扩展，为研究更高维拓扑不变量提供了新工具。

Abstract: For a commutative Frobenius algebra $A$, we construct a $(2,3,3+\varepsilon)$-dimensional TQFT $\mathsf{AFK}_A$ that assigns to a 3-manifold a skein module of embedded $A$-decorated surfaces. These surface skein modules have been first defined by Asaeda--Frohman and Kaiser using skein relations that generalize the combinatorics of Bar-Natan's dotted cobordisms. For 3-manifolds with boundary, we show that surface skein modules carry an action by a certain surface skein category associated with the boundary, which yields a gluing formalism. Our main result concerns a partial extension of $\mathsf{AFK}_A$ to dimension 4, which uses an inductive state-sum construction following Walker. As an example, the equivariant version of Lee's deformation of dotted cobordisms yields a TQFT that extends to 4-dimensional 2-handlebodies but not 3-handlebodies. Finally, we characterize the attachment of 4-dimensional 2-handles by means of a certain Kirby color and use it to compute the invariants of 4-dimensional 2-handlebodies in examples.

### [2] [Generalized Schur limit, modular differential equations and quantum monodromy traces](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.02102)
*Anirudh Deb*

Main category: hep-th

TL;DR: 该论文探讨广义Schur极限的性质，发现其作为α的函数满足固定阶数的模线性微分方程，并在某些负整数α值时与量子单值算子的高次幂迹重合，暗示了Coulomb分支与Higgs分支之间的对应关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究广义Schur极限的数学性质，探索其在Argyres-Douglas理论中的特殊表现，试图建立Coulomb分支上的wall-crossing不变量与Higgs分支上的广义Schur极限之间的对应关系。

Method: 基于arXiv:2506.13764中定义的广义Schur极限，通过多个具体例子进行探索性研究，观察其数学性质并形成猜想。

Result: 1. 猜想广义Schur极限作为α的函数满足固定阶数的模线性微分方程，系数依赖于α；2. 对于(A₁,G)型Argyres-Douglas理论（G=Aₙ,Dₙ），在某些负整数α值时，广义Schur极限与量子单值算子高次幂的迹重合。

Conclusion: 广义Schur极限与量子单值算子迹的对应关系暗示了Coulomb分支上的wall-crossing不变量与Higgs分支上的广义Schur极限之间存在更一般的联系，这为理解两个分支之间的关系提供了新线索。

Abstract: We explore some aspects of the generalized Schur limit, defined in arXiv:2506.13764. Based on several examples, we conjecture that the generalized Schur limit as a function of $α$ solves a modular linear differential equation of fixed order, with coefficients depending on $α$. We also observe in examples that for Argyres-Douglas theories of type $(A_1,G)$ with $G=A_n,D_n$, the generalized Schur limit for certain negative integer values of $α$, coincides with the trace of higher powers of the quantum monodromy operator. This hints at a more general correspondence between the wall-crossing invariant traces on the Coulomb branch and the generalized Schur limit, which is related to the Higgs branch.

</details>
