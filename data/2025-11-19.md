### [1] [Periods in equivariant and motivic contexts](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.14325)
*Martin Gallauer*

Main category: math.CT

TL;DR: 本文定义了稳定对称幺半∞-范畴的周期作为乘法特征，建立了其基本性质，并研究了多个例子，重点关注'普通'等变和动机同伦理论。应用发现于动机tt-几何中的各向同性点。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究稳定对称幺半∞-范畴的乘法特征——周期，旨在理解等变和动机同伦理论中的周期性现象，并将其应用于动机tt-几何中的各向同性点研究。

Method: 定义稳定对称幺半∞-范畴的周期概念，发展其基本理论性质，通过多个具体例子进行验证和分析，特别关注等变和动机同伦理论中的应用。

Result: 建立了周期理论的基本框架，展示了在等变和动机同伦理论中的具体表现，并将这些发现成功应用于动机tt-几何中各向同性点的研究。

Conclusion: 周期作为稳定对称幺半∞-范畴的重要乘法特征，在等变和动机同伦理论中具有广泛应用，特别在动机tt-几何的各向同性点研究中展现出重要价值。

Abstract: We define the period as a multiplicative characteristic of stably symmetric monoidal $\infty$-categories, develop its basic properties, and study many examples, with a focus on `ordinary' equivariant and motivic homotopy theory. We apply the findings to isotropic points in motivic tt-geometry. (Includes an appendix by Ivo Dell'Ambrogio on generalized comparison maps in tt-geometry.)

### [2] [The semi-stable Local Langlands Correspondence](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.14382)
*Eknath Ghate*

Main category: math.NT

TL;DR: 本文总结了模p局部朗兰兹对应的Iwahori理论重构，并利用p进函数分析工具构建了与二维半稳定Galois表示相关的p进Banach空间。通过计算该空间中格的约化，结合Iwahori模p LLC，完全确定了当p≥5时，所有权重3≤k≤p+1和所有L-不变量的模p约化。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究二维半稳定Galois表示的模p约化问题，通过结合Iwahori模p局部朗兰兹对应和p进函数分析工具，为理解这类表示的约化行为提供系统方法。

Method: 采用Iwahori理论重构模p局部朗兰兹对应，利用p进函数分析定义与Galois表示相关的p进Banach空间，通过计算格约化结合Iwahori模p LLC确定模p约化。

Result: 对于p≥5，完全确定了所有权重3≤k≤p+1和所有L-不变量的二维半稳定Galois表示的模p约化。

Conclusion: 该方法成功结合了Iwahori理论和p进分析工具，为研究Galois表示的模p约化提供了有效框架，特别适用于特定参数范围内的二维半稳定表示。

Abstract: We start with background that goes into an Iwahori-theoretic reformulation of the mod $p$ Local Langlands Correspondence (§2). We then explain some classical $p$-adic functional analytic results (§3) that go into defining the $p$-adic Banach space (§4) attached to a two-dimensional semi-stable representation $V_{k,{\mathcal L}}$ of the Galois group of ${\mathbb Q}_p$ of weight $k$ and ${\mathcal L}$-invariant ${\mathcal L}$ under the $p$-adic Local Langlands correspondence. We then sketch how to compute the reduction of a lattice in this Banach space, which along with the Iwahori mod $p$ LLC, allows one to completely determine the mod $p$ reduction of $V_{k,{\mathcal L}}$ for all weights $3 \leq k \leq p+1$ and all ${\mathcal L}$ for $p \geq 5$ (§5). These notes are a summary of our joint work with Anand Chitrao [CG24]. Emphasis is placed on motivation and background rather than completeness.

</details>

Main category: math.RT

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>

Abstract: Let $A$ be a Nakayama algebra. Using Ringel's resolution quiver, we give a criterion to decide whether $A$ is minimal Auslander-Gorenstein. The criterion strongly relies on the parity of the selfinjective dimension of $A$.

### [4] [Covering Barbasch-Vogan duality and wavefront sets of genuine representations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.14750)
*Fan Gao,Baiying Liu,Chi-Heng Lo,Freydoon Shahidi*

Main category: math.RT

TL;DR: 本文定义了覆盖Barbasch-Vogan对偶性并证明其性质，为p-adic覆盖群的真表示提出了波前集上界猜想，并将其简化为反离散表示，最后证明了广义线性群Kazhdan-Patterson覆盖的该猜想。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 将Ciubotaru-Kim和Hazeltine-Liu-Lo-Shahidi为线性代数群提出的波前集上界猜想推广到p-adic覆盖群的真表示。

Method: 定义覆盖Barbasch-Vogan对偶性并证明其性质，利用该对偶性为p-adic覆盖群的真表示构建波前集上界猜想，并将问题简化为反离散表示。

Result: 证明了广义线性群Kazhdan-Patterson覆盖的波前集上界猜想。

Conclusion: 成功将波前集上界理论从线性代数群推广到p-adic覆盖群，并为Kazhdan-Patterson覆盖建立了完整的理论框架。

Abstract: In this paper, we start by defining a covering Barbasch-Vogan duality and prove some of its properties. Then, for genuine representations of $p$-adic covering groups we formulate an upper bound conjecture for their wavefront sets using this covering Barbasch-Vogan duality and reduce it to anti-discrete representations. The formulation generalizes that of Ciubotaru-Kim and Hazeltine-Liu-Lo-Shahidi for linear algebraic groups. We prove this upper bound conjecture for Kazhdan-Patterson coverings of general linear groups.

</details>

*Changjian Fu,Haicheng Zhang*

Main category: math.QA

TL;DR: 本文构建了量子群 U⁺_v(g) 到具有主系数的量子丛代数 A_q 的代数同态，并证明了一步突变产生的量子丛变量满足量子 Serre 关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究量子群与量子丛代数之间的代数结构关系，探索量子丛变量在量子群框架下的代数性质。

Method: 通过构造代数同态映射，分析量子丛代数中一步突变产生的量子丛变量，验证其满足量子 Serre 关系。

Result: 成功建立了量子群到量子丛代数的同态映射，并证明了一步突变量子丛变量满足高阶量子 Serre 关系。

Conclusion: 量子丛代数与量子群之间存在深刻的代数结构联系，量子丛变量继承了量子群的代数性质。

Abstract: We provide a homomorphism of algebras from the quantum group $\mathbf{U}^+_v(\mathfrak{g})$ to the corresponding quantum cluster algebra $\mathcal {A}_q$ with principal coefficients. As a by-product, we show that the quantum cluster variables arising from one-step mutations from the initial cluster variables satisfy the (high order) quantum Serre relations in $\mathcal {A}_q$.

### [6] [Cluster scattering diagrams via quiver moduli and tight gradings](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.14672)
*Amanda Burcroff,Kyungyong Lee,Lang Mou,Gregg Musiker,Markus Reineke*

Main category: math.CO

TL;DR: 本文通过模空间和紧分级框架研究秩2聚类散射图，证明了Elgin-Reading-Stella关于壁函数系数结构和计数性质的猜想，并提供了散射图Weyl群对称性的新证明。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究秩2聚类散射图的结构和计数性质，验证Elgin-Reading-Stella提出的相关猜想，并从新的角度理解散射图的对称性。

Method: 结合模空间理论和紧分级组合框架，使用箭袋表示和组合方法分析散射图。

Result: 证明了壁函数系数的结构和枚举性质猜想，并给出了散射图Weyl群对称性的新证明。

Conclusion: 紧分级视角为研究聚类散射图提供了有效方法，成功验证了相关猜想并深化了对散射图对称性的理解。

Abstract: We study rank-2 cluster scattering diagrams through moduli spaces of quiver representations and a recently developed combinatorial framework of tight gradings. Combining quiver-theoretic and combinatorial methods, we prove and extend a collection of conjectures posed by Elgin--Reading--Stella concerning the structural and enumerative properties of the wall-function coefficients. The tight grading perspective also provides a new proof of the Weyl group symmetry of the scattering diagram.

</details>
